"MULTI-DISCIPLINARY DESIGN COMBINES AN EMPATHETIC AND CURIOUS WAY OF SEEING THE WORLD WITH RIGOROUS METHODS OF OBSERVATION, IDEATION, AND PRODUCTION

I was born in London England, educated at Anglia Ruskin University in Cambridge & currently reside in Denmark. After spending numerous years working alongside a number of creative individuals, I found myself inpsired to create also. In 2019, I decided to make a major decision to re-edutate myself on the subject of Design

Born in London, England to parents of caribean descent, I personally don't think I was the most creative child. Instead, most of my time was spent playing football. It wasn't until university when I first began to explore my creative side. Whilst Studying Social Policy at Anglia Ruskin University, I decided to take some time to also study fashion design at The London College of Fashion. This would be my first taste of creativity.

Following Graduation from University in Social Policy, I was unsure whether I had chosen the right path. As a result I began to look for new areas of expertise whilst traveling the world. This Is how I began working in marketing in Munich, Germany. I began learning the ropes at a number of valuable internship positions including Becoacht, Sixt & Internations. Most notably during my time in Munich, I worked with the extremely talented team at COBE. Doing so was a great experience as I was able to put all the skills I had learnt into practice. Furthermore, I was able to do so in an environment that welcomed creativity and innovation.

Following My departure from Munich, I briefly returned to the UK to tie the knot with my wife.
